Who we are

TransferUp was launched in 2024 with the vision of making international money transfers cheap, fair, and simple. Today, our multi-currency account helps millions of people and businesses manage their money across the world. Here’s how we got here.

Our Values

Our Mission

TransferUp core mission is centered around revolutionizing the financial industry by providing fast, transparent, and cost-effective money transfer services across borders. They strive to empower individuals and businesses to manage their finances seamlessly, breaking down the barriers of traditional banking systems.

Our Vision

Empowering global financial connectivity, TransferUp envisions a borderless world where swift, transparent, and cost-effective money transfers redefine financial accessibility. We strive to lead this transformation, leveraging innovation to break down traditional barriers and make financial empowerment a reality for individuals and businesses worldwide.

Our Goal

Our goal at TransferUp is to revolutionize cross-border financial transactions, offering swift, transparent, and cost-effective services. We aim to empower individuals and businesses, breaking down traditional barriers and fostering financial inclusivity on a global scale.
